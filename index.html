A Rhythm Cooking RPG

Step into the world of Niya–A med school dropout with hopes of becoming a world class chef! Cook dishes from all over the world to the beat of the music, explore the town and make friends!

Chef Niya is a rhythm cooking RPG Where the player follows Niya on her journey to become a fully fledged chef.
Become the owner of the best food cart in chef Niya! Go on a life changing journey to the art district, cook mouth watering dishes, make new friends, and conquer the challenges of being a small business! In this wonderfully cozy, rhythm cooking RPG.

15 years ago, Niya’s father gifted her the family recipe book. It had three recipes, but Niya was 10 and it meant a lot. Today, Niya is leaving med school to become a chef. Still with only three recipes and a very old food cart, she dreams of running the best food cart in the art district. However, she’s got to make it there, discover new recipes, and make connections first. Good Luck Niya!

  • Experience the thrill of rhythm-based cooking challenges!
  • Unlock new recipes and ingredients as you progress through the game!
  • Cook dishes from different cultures all around the world!
  • Explore the Art District and meet new people!
